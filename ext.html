About Biswajit Sarkar

Biswajit Sarkar is one of India's most trusted patient guides, dedicated to helping families find the right treatment at the right hospital within their budget. He is the founder of Chennai Medical Guidance, an initiative that has supported more than 15,000 patients over the last 10 years.

His journey began when his own daughter fell seriously ill. Lost and confused in an unfamiliar city, he struggled to find proper medical guidance in Chennai. That moment made him realize that thousands of families face the same challenge‚Äîand some even lose their loved ones simply due to lack of information.

Driven by this experience, Biswajit Sarkar built a network of 200+ hospitals and 1,000+ trusted doctors, ensuring that every patient receives honest guidance, the best possible care, and complete support during their medical journey. Today, he continues to stand beside patients across India, helping them make informed healthcare decisions with confidence.
